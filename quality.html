Our Process

Quality Control Steps

01
Incoming Material Inspection
Every raw material batch is tested for conductivity, purity, and dimensional accuracy before entering production. Non-conforming materials are immediately rejected.
02
In-Process Quality Control
Dedicated QC personnel perform random sampling at each manufacturing stage — cutting, welding, pressing and coating — to catch deviations early.
03
Dimensional Verification
CMM (Coordinate Measuring Machine) inspection verifies all critical dimensions on complex parts to ±0.001mm accuracy, ensuring perfect fit in assembly.
04
100% Hi-Pot Testing
Every single insulated product undergoes high-potential dielectric strength testing at up to 10kV AC to verify insulation integrity before shipment.
05
Resistance & Conductivity Test
DC resistance measurement and conductivity verification ensure each busbar meets the specified electrical performance requirements for its application.
06
Final Inspection & Traceability
Full visual and mechanical inspection of finished goods. Every batch receives a unique traceability code linking it to raw material certificates and production records.
